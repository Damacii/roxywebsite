« About me »

Founder & Social Media Manager.

Hi, I'm Roxanna, first gen Latina. After 14 years in the automotive industry, I followed my heart into the creative space. It started with my own podcast— learning, testing, and loving the process of telling stories through words, photos, and video. A few good friends noticed my eye for detail and encouraged me to turn this into a side business. That spark led me to helping brands and creators bring their visions to life. Now I work with busy entrepreneurs who want and need a helping hand to build real connection online.

I build content that sounds like you, and actually moves the needle-I'm all about growth, authenticity and turning your goals into tangible outcomes you can be proud of. If you're looking for genuine engagement, a consistent voice, and someone who's excited to grow with you, let's connect and create something you love.
