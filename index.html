« About me »

Founder & Social Media Manager.

Hi, I’m Roxanna. After 13 years in the car industry, I followed my heart into the creative space. It started with my own podcast—learning, testing, and loving the process of telling stories through words, photos, and video. A good friend noticed my eye for detail and encouraged me to turn this into a side business. That spark led me to helping brands and creators bring their visions to life. Now I work with busy people who want and need a helping hand to build real connection online.

I build content that sounds like you, and actually moves the needle—I’m all about growth, authenticity and turning your goals into tangible outcomes you can be proud of. If you’re looking for genuine engagement, a consistent voice, and someone who’s excited to grow with you, let’s connect and create something you love.
